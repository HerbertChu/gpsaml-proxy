A self-hosted bastion that runs your GlobalProtect tunnel in the cloud and exposes corp services to your laptop over plain SSH. No openconnect on your machine, no sudo prompt, no Hardened-Runtime fights — just one sshuttle command.

press ↵ · takes ~12 seconds · key valid for the session only

what happens when you click

  1. Redirect to your IdP (Microsoft Entra) and complete MFA
  2. Bastion calls prelogin.esp & getconfig.esp on the GP portal with your SAML assertion
  3. openconnect spawns inside a private network namespace allocated to your account
  4. A fresh ed25519 key is generated; the public half lands in your authorized_keys on this host
  5. The private half is offered to your browser as a one-time download — never written to disk on the bastion

this host has access to

  • Your SAML username, group memberships, and the GP authcookie returned by the portal
  • The packets your sshuttle session forwards while you're connected
  • The public half of the SSH key it issued to you

this host never sees

  • Your IdP password or MFA factor — those stay between your browser and Microsoft Entra
  • The private SSH key after it's been sent to your browser (deleted on issue)
  • Anything sshuttle isn't asked to forward — local laptop traffic never touches this bastion
